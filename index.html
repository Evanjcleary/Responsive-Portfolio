About Me


My name is Evan Cleary. I was born in an outskirts of Philadelphia called Ambler, PA. I am one of 6 siblings in my family. I have 1 twin, 2 brothers, 3 sisters and 60 cousins. All of them are older then me, including my twin. She lives far away now.

I graduated college in 2014 where I was President of the service organization I was a part of. I then was selected to be a Business developer for a Bio-Tech start-up. I was tasked with running the seed-funding initiative, which involved presenting the prototype and business plan to Angel Investors. I later went on to start my own company which is steady and growing. Though this particular business, while profitable, is not fulfilling my creative aspirations.

Growing up I've had many ideas for utilizations of technology. I have notebooks of ideas stacked in my home, but never the tools or means to execute on them. I plan to learn everything I can about computer programming so I can build more start-up companies. I hope to make many different web applications and mobile applications with my skillset to start to build a solid holding company that generates revenue from these minor applications.

Then, I have one idea in particular that I believe can be very popular. So I intend to work as hard as I can until I develop the skillset necessary to carry out that idea.
